FK: Foreign Key
ZF: Zero Fill (unsigned)

| User
==================================

varchar(255)  | Email
varchar(100)  | Name
varchar(100)  | Password

| Note
==================================
bigint PK ZF  | ID
tinyint FK ZF | StatusID
varchar(100)  | Title
text          | Description (Markdown)
datetime      | CreatedDateTime
datetime      | EditedDateTime
bigint FK ZF  | CreatedByUserID
bigint FK ZF  | EditedByUserID
tinyint ZF    | ColorID

| Snippet
==================================

| Image
